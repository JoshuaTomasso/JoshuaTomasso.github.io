Overview

Lizard Wizard is a Unity game where my primary contribution was the sound system. Audio work is often undervalued, but it directly impacts player feedback, game readability, and the overall feel of moment-to-moment play.

My work involved building the audio architecture in C#, wiring sound triggers to gameplay events, and ensuring audio responses were clean and maintainable as the project grew.

  • Audio system design and implementation in Unity using C#
  • Gameplay event-driven sound triggering
  • Player feedback and game feel through audio
  • Sound organization and maintainability
